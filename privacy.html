Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Climby ("we", "our", "us") is a social competition platform that allows users to create and participate in creative challenges. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Controller: [Your Full Legal Name], [Your Address]

Contact: climbysocial@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address, username (handle), avatar image, bio, country, and age consent confirmation.

User Content: Photos, videos, and audio recordings submitted to challenges; messages sent through the chat feature; votes and reactions.

Usage Data: App interactions, screen views, session duration, and feature usage analytics.

Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Contract Performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): To provide and maintain the Climby platform and your account.
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): To improve our services, ensure security, and prevent abuse.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): For optional features like push notifications and analytics.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the app
  • Display your profile and submissions to other users
  • Process votes and calculate leaderboard rankings
  • Send push notifications (with your consent)
  • Moderate content and enforce community guidelines
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (EU — Frankfurt, Germany): Database, authentication, file storage, and real-time messaging.
  • Firebase (Google, US): Push notifications (FCM), crash reporting (Crashlytics), analytics, and remote configuration.
  • Resend (US): Email delivery for authentication OTP codes via noreply@climbyapp.com.

6. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in the EU (Supabase, Frankfurt). Some data is processed by US-based services (Firebase, Resend) under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure adequate data protection.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you deactivate your account, your data is hidden but preserved for 90 days in case you wish to reactivate. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed, though anonymized content (challenges, submissions) may be retained.

8. Children's Privacy

Climby requires users to meet minimum age requirements based on their country:

  • United States: 13 years (COPPA compliance)
  • EEA/UK: 14 years (GDPR compliance)
  • All other countries: 18 years

We do not knowingly collect data from users below the applicable minimum age. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data (right to be forgotten) — available via account deletion in the app
  • Port your data to another service
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at climbysocial@gmail.com.

10. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS/SSL), secure authentication, and row-level security policies on our database. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of Climby after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us
